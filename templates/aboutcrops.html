Crops Information

Wheat

Wheat is one of the most widely grown cereal crops in the world. It is a staple food for billions of people and is used to make various products such as bread, pasta, and breakfast cereals.

Rice

Rice is a major food crop and a primary source of nourishment for more than half of the world's population. It is cultivated in diverse environments and is consumed in various forms.

Maize (Corn)

Maize, also known as corn, is one of the most important cereal crops globally. It is used for food, animal feed, and industrial purposes. Maize cultivation is widespread due to its adaptability to different climatic conditions.

Soybeans

Soybeans are a versatile crop with various uses, including food, animal feed, and biofuel production. They are rich in protein and oil, making them valuable in both human and animal diets.

Potatoes

Potatoes are a staple food in many parts of the world and are consumed in numerous dishes. They are a good source of carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals and can be grown in diverse climates.
